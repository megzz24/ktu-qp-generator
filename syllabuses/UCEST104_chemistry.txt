COURSE CODE: UCEST104
SUBJECT: Chemistry
============================================================

MODULE 1: Electrochemistry and Corrosion
--------------------------------------------------
  - Electrochemical cell, electrode potential, Nernst equation (single electrode and cell)
  - Reference electrodes: SHE and Calomel electrode, construction and working
  - Electrochemical series, applications
  - Glass electrode and pH measurement
  - Conductivity measurement using digital conductivity meter
  - Li-ion battery and H2-O2 fuel cell (acid electrolyte): construction and working
  - Corrosion: electrochemical mechanism (acidic and alkaline medium)
  - Galvanic series, corrosion control methods
  - Cathodic protection: sacrificial anodic and impressed current
  - Electroplating and electroless plating of copper

MODULE 2: Materials for Electronic Applications
--------------------------------------------------
  - Nanomaterials: classification by dimension and material
  - Synthesis: sol-gel and chemical reduction
  - Carbon nanotubes, fullerenes, graphene, carbon quantum dots: structure, properties, applications
  - Fire retardant polymers: halogenated and non-halogenated
  - Conducting polymers: polyaniline and polypyrrole, synthesis, properties, applications
  - OLED: construction, working, applications
  - Dye-sensitized solar cells (DSSC): construction, working, applications
  - Materials for quantum computing, supercapacitors, spintronics

MODULE 3: Spectroscopy and Analytical Techniques
--------------------------------------------------
  - Spectroscopy: types of spectra, molecular energy levels
  - Beer-Lambert's law, numerical problems
  - Electronic spectroscopy: principle, types of electronic transitions, conjugation effect
  - UV-Vis instrumentation and applications
  - Vibrational spectroscopy: principle and applications
  - Thermal analysis: Dielectric Thermal Analysis (DETA) of polymers
  - Electron microscopy: SEM — principle, instrumentation, applications

MODULE 4: Environmental Chemistry and Water Treatment
--------------------------------------------------
  - Water hardness: temporary and permanent, disadvantages, degree of hardness
  - Water softening: ion exchange process, reverse osmosis
  - Water disinfection: chlorination, break-point chlorination, ozone, UV irradiation
  - Dissolved oxygen (DO), BOD, COD: definition and significance
  - Sewage treatment: primary, secondary, tertiary, trickling filter, UASB process
  - E-waste: disposal methods — recycle, recovery, reuse
  - Climate change: greenhouse gases, ozone depletion
  - Sustainable development and SDGs
