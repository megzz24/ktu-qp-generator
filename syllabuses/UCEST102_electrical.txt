COURSE CODE: UCEST102
SUBJECT: Electrical and Electronics
============================================================

MODULE 1: DC Circuits and Magnetic Circuits
--------------------------------------------------
  - Current and voltage division rule, relative potential
  - Capacitors and inductors: V-I relations, energy stored
  - Ohm's law, Kirchhoff's laws - numerical problems
  - Star-delta conversion (resistive networks) - numerical problems
  - Mesh current method: matrix representation, solution of network equations
  - Node voltage method: matrix representation, solution of network equations by matrix methods, numerical problems
  - Magnetic circuits: MMF, field strength, flux density, reluctance
  - Comparison of electric and magnetic circuits
  - Series and parallel magnetic circuits with composite materials (numerical problems not needed)

MODULE 2: AC Circuits and Three Phase Systems
--------------------------------------------------
  - Electromagnetic induction: Faraday's laws, Lenz's law
  - Statically and dynamically induced EMF
  - Self-inductance, mutual inductance, coefficient of coupling
  - AC generation, sinusoidal waveforms: frequency, period, average value, RMS, form factor
  - Phasor representation: trigonometric, rectangular, polar, complex forms
  - Purely resistive, inductive and capacitive circuits
  - Inductive and capacitive reactance, impedance
  - RL, RC, RLC series circuits: power factor, active, reactive, apparent power
  - Three phase systems: generation, advantages, star and delta connections
  - Line and phase voltages and currents (balanced only)

MODULE 3: Electronic Devices and Transistors
--------------------------------------------------
  - Passive and active components
  - PN junction diode: working, V-I characteristics
  - Zener diode, avalanche breakdown, Zener voltage regulator
  - DC power supply block diagram
  - Half wave, full wave, bridge rectifiers, ripple factor, capacitor filter
  - BJT: construction, working, V-I characteristics, CE configuration
  - CE, CB, CC configuration comparison
  - Biasing concept, load line
  - Transistor as switch and amplifier
  - RC coupled amplifier: circuit diagram, frequency response
  - FET, N-channel and P-channel MOSFETs: construction and working

MODULE 4: Modern Electronics and Communication
--------------------------------------------------
  - Communication system block diagram
  - Fiber optic communication system block diagram
  - AM and FM concepts, AM and FM superheterodyne receiver block diagram
  - Wired and wireless communication basics
  - Comparison of 3G, 4G, 5G, 6G technologies
  - Electronic instrumentation system, digital multimeter, function generator
  - CRO and Lissajous patterns
  - IoT applications: smart homes, healthcare, agriculture
