SUBJECT: Physics
============================================================

MODULE 1: Electrical Conductivity and Superconductivity
--------------------------------------------------
  - Classical free electron theory, electrical conductivity in metals
  - Fermi-Dirac distribution, variation of Fermi function with temperature
  - Fermi energy, energy bands
  - Classification of materials: conductor, semiconductor, insulator
  - Superconductivity: transition temperature, critical field, Meissner effect
  - Type I and Type II superconductors
  - BCS theory, applications of superconductors

MODULE 2: Quantum Mechanics
--------------------------------------------------
  - Introduction to quantum mechanics
  - Uncertainty principle: statement and applications
  - Absence of electron inside nucleus, natural line broadening
  - Wave function: properties and physical interpretation
  - Time dependent and time independent Schrodinger equations
  - Particle in a one-dimensional box: energy eigenvalues, normalized wave function
  - Quantum mechanical tunnelling (qualitative)

MODULE 3: Semiconductor Physics
--------------------------------------------------
  - Intrinsic semiconductor
  - Density of electrons in conduction band and holes in valence band
  - Intrinsic carrier concentration and variation with temperature
  - Extrinsic semiconductor (qualitative)
  - Formation of p-n junction
  - Fermi level in intrinsic and extrinsic semiconductors
  - Energy band diagram of p-n junction
  - Charge flow across p-n junction, forward and reverse bias
  - Diode equation derivation, I-V characteristics

MODULE 4: Semiconductor Devices
--------------------------------------------------
  - Half wave and full wave rectifiers
  - Zener diode: V-I characteristics
  - Tunnel diode: V-I characteristics
  - Semiconductor laser: construction, working, applications
  - Photodetectors: junction photodiode
  - Solar cell: working, V-I characteristics, solar panel
  - Light emitting diode (LED): working and applications
